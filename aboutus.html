Why Sharknado?

Sharknado HQ is where you will find all the cutting edge Shark technologies. Our team has worked very hard to bring you the best of the best. Sharknado was formed from an intense collaboration of thrill seekers, bent on diving into the world of shark attacks on land. Each foray with the toothy beasts exposed challenges to be conquered. With a our new and trusted weapons of github, we pounded out and navigated a way to success. Currently accepting applications for underwater bait and chum associates.
